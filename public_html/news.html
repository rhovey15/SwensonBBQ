Coca-Cola BBQ!

Did you know you can make BBQ sauce with Coca-Cola?? It is crazy all you need is a few simple ingredients and a place to make it! This recipe is perfect for the summer or any sporting event at the house. It is so easy a monkey can do it! All you need to do is mix some ingredients tougher. The ingredients are(1can of Coca-Cola, ketchup, apple cider vinegar, and a few spices to get the taste you love. There is only one catch to this recipe and it is that you need to let it sit for a day so it can really attach its self to the chicken.

10 Minute Dinner

Busy moms and busy dads sometimes do not have enough time to prepare a fancy dinner but at the same time you don't want your children to always be eating frozen pizza or McDonald's. This is a quick and easy recipe that all families will love. The ingredients are( 3/4 cup ketchup, 2 Tbsps brown sugar, 2 Tbsps. honey, 1Tbsps. Dijon mustard, 1 tsp. smoked paprika, 1/2 tsp. garlic powder, black pepper to taste, and 4 (8-ounce) bone-in port chops. You will mix all of these ingredients tougher and rub them on the pork chop. After that cook them in the over for 5 minutes on each side.
